You asked, we answered

Commonly asked questions

Feel free to reach out with any questions you might have.

Is it really unlimited?

Yep! Think of Autoolize like a buffet - you can come back for seconds and thirds, but it's one plate at a time before we move on to the next course. So, grab a seat and let us serve up some design magic!

Why wouldn't I just hire a full-time expert?

Great question! First off, hiring a full-time, senior-level automation and AI expert can cost over $100,000 a year, plus benefits (and that's if you can even find one who's available). Moreover, you might not have enough consistent work to keep them fully engaged, leaving you paying for downtime.

With our monthly subscription, however, you have the flexibility to pause and resume your service as needed, ensuring you're only paying when there's actual work to be done.

How long does it take?

On average, most requests are completed in just two days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer.

Who are the experts?

It might come as a surprise, but Autoolize is actually a one-person agency. This means you'll be working directly with me, the founder of Autoolize.

How do we communicate?

We provide ample flexibility with how you can send in your requests through Trello. Whether it's directly on Trello, through Google Docs or PDFs, or even a quick Loom video for those who'd rather not write everything down, we've got you covered. Essentially, if it can be linked to or shared on Trello, we welcome it.

Can I pause, renew or cancel at any time?

You're in control! Should you need to take a break from our journey together, just let us know, and we'll hit pause for you. Your subscription will be here waiting whenever you decide to come back (we'll keep the light on for you). And if you're ready to part ways, we'll hold back the tears. The memories we've made will always be cherished.
